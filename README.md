## About MARGDARSHAK

MARGDARSHAK is an all-in-one student management system designed for online learning. Our mission is to boost productivity and academic success by providing a comprehensive suite of tools for students and educators.

---

## Features

- **Task Manager:** Organize your project tasks, homework, and assignments with our powerful to-do list app.
- **Timetable Creator:** Create and manage your class schedule, weekly planner, and daily routines with ease.
- **Grade Tracker:** Keep track of your grades, manage your online gradebook, and calculate your GPA.
- **Note-Taking App:** Take and organize class notes in a collaborative digital notebook.
- **Study Timer:** Boost your focus and productivity with an integrated Pomodoro timer.
- **And much more!** Including a progress tracker, attendance monitor, and a library of educational resources.
