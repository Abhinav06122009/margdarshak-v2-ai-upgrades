Core Features for Academic Excellence

1. Advanced Grade Tracking

Understanding your academic standing is crucial. Our Grade Tracker allows you to input weighted grades for assignments, quizzes, and exams. It automatically calculates your current GPA and predicts what scores you need on future exams to maintain your desired average. This proactive approach prevents end-of-semester surprises.

2. Task Management & To-Do Lists

Never miss a deadline again. Our Task Manager is built specifically for students. You can tag assignments by course, set priority levels (High, Medium, Low), and visualize your workload using our Kanban board view. Breaking down large projects into smaller, manageable sub-tasks helps overcome procrastination.

3. Pomodoro Study Timer

Focus is a resource. Our integrated Pomodoro Timer uses the scientifically proven technique of working in 25-minute intervals separated by short breaks. This method prevents burnout and keeps your mind fresh during long study sessions. You can customize the intervals to match your personal flow state.

4. Scientific Calculator

Mathematics and science students need reliable tools. Our Scientific Calculator is accessible directly from your dashboard. It supports advanced functions including trigonometry (sin, cos, tan), logarithms, exponentials, and factorials. Having this tool integrated saves you from switching tabs or carrying a physical device.

5. Course & Syllabus Management

Keep your academic life organized by digitizing your syllabus. Track professor contact information, office hours, and class locations all in one place. Our system sends reminders for upcoming lectures and changes in your timetable.
